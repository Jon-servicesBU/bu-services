Friday Salesforce Update — Standing Requirement

Every Friday by 4:00 PM ET  ·  All commercial owners  ·  Power Automate runs Sunday 7:00 PM ET

Why this matters: Power Automate runs every Sunday at 7:00 PM ET — it reads Salesforce and pushes data into the Command Centre and Monday email digest. The Friday 4:00 PM deadline provides a 27-hour buffer. Stale Salesforce = stale Command Centre = stale Monday meeting = stale Eurazeo reporting.

Owner Responsibilities

Every Friday by 4:00 PM
Vinoth Tharma
Mining NDT (East) + Nuclear/OPG + 3X Composite. All site visit quotes, new account proposals, signed inspection agreements.
Max Aggrey
Mining NDT (West) + 3X. All Alberta and BC opportunities. Any new utility conversations opened this week.
Rick Carlton
Pipeline DA. All Pembina and Tidewater opportunities. Signed dig programs marked Closed Won. Joel Carlton flags field work by Thursday.
Mathieu Beauchesne
Powerline. Hydro-Québec and utility pipeline opportunities. Any new utility conversations.
Olivier Marcotte
Nuclear/OPG executive-level deals. Confirm all initiative opportunities have correct BU Initiative picklist values.

Checklist

Every Friday
  • All deals closed this week → Stage = Closed Won. Amount in CAD. Close Date = invoice date.
  • All new prospects → New Opportunity with BU_Initiative__c picklist value set.
  • All active opportunities → Stage and Amount updated to reflect current status.
  • New account relationships → Create Account first, then the Opportunity.
  • Ops-to-commercial handoff → If ops started a new engagement, commercial owner has a Salesforce opportunity open by Friday.
Escalation: If a commercial owner has not updated Salesforce by Friday 4:00 PM, Jon Eustace contacts them directly before end of day. This is not discretionary — the accuracy of the Command Centre, Monday digest, and Eurazeo quarterly reporting depends on it.
