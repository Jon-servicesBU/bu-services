MSA and maintenance/inspection (Vinoth's track with Jon): OPG and Bruce Power ongoing outage inspection and maintenance contracts. Vinoth Tharma owns the commercial pipeline; Jon Eustace supports with his OPG executive relationship. Your role: crew readiness and outage mobilisation support for Joel Djordjevic's Central team.
You contribute to both tracks operationally — but your primary commercial partner is Olivier on the new build/refurb side. If an existing Nuclear client raises a new scope — flag it to Vinoth or Olivier immediately, depending on which track.

03 Programme Management hide ▴
Crew qualification and readiness: Maintain a current register of all Nucleom technicians with nuclear-sector qualifications — NDT levels, OPG vendor approvals, CANDU-specific training. Identify qualification gaps relative to Darlington and Pickering scopes and build a remediation plan. OPG vendor approval processes take months — start well ahead of any outage or execution window.
Bid support for new build and refurb scopes: When Olivier prepares bids for Aecon/OPG nuclear scopes, you provide the operational input: crew names, qualification evidence, methodology, and mobilisation plan. You are the technical credibility behind the commercial proposal.
Outage window readiness — MSA track: For OPG maintenance and inspection outages (Vinoth's track), ensure Joel Djordjevic's Central team has the right crew available and qualified before each outage window opens. Review the Nuclear Outage Calendar in the Command Centre regularly — flag any readiness concerns at least 90 days ahead.
