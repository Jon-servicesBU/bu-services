Sequencing Logic — Why Everything Runs in Parallel

All five initiatives launch simultaneously. Mining and Pipeline generate 2026 cash flow that funds Nuclear MSA tender costs. The Pipeline rig buildout (Q2) is the largest single CAPEX event and the most time-critical — Tidewater summer dig program is a hard window. Nuclear MSA preparation runs through all of 2026 as a Q3 2026 submission target. 3X Composite is deliberately low-investment in 2026 — ABSA re-submission is the only blocker, and non-pressure revenue (tanks, mining) is available now without it.

Contingency & Pivot Plans
Three scenarios that materially change the plan — trigger conditions, pivot decisions, and revised forecasts
How Contingencies Are Structured

Each scenario defines: (1) the trigger condition — the specific event or signal that activates the contingency, (2) immediate actions within 30 days of trigger, (3) the revised revenue forecast if the contingency plays out fully, and (4) the pivot — how BU Services redeploys the resources and energy that were allocated to the lost initiative into alternative growth vectors. No scenario puts BU Services in existential risk — the base business generates $47.2M regardless.
