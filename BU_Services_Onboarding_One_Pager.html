The business

What is BU Services?

BU Services is the commercial and operational platform that sits above two NDT companies: Nucleom Inc. (employed by, largest entity) and Nortech Advanced NDT. Together they deliver industrial inspection, data analytics, and composite repair services across Canada.

Jon Eustace is CEO North America at Ekoscan Integrity Group (EIG) — the parent company. His mandate is to hit a $92.8M revenue target by 2030 across five growth initiatives, while running the base business of both companies.

Eurazeo is EIG's investor. Jon reports quarterly to Eurazeo on revenue, EBITDA, CAPEX, and initiative progress. This shapes how the whole commercial system is built — everything feeds into the Eurazeo reporting pack.

Non-negotiable rules

5 Things to Know

1
Commercial and ops are separate. Ops directors do not prospect, attend conferences for BD, or manage new business pipelines. New opportunities go to the commercial owner the same day they arise.
2
Salesforce by Friday 4PM, every week. Every deal, every new prospect, every update. If it's not in Salesforce, it doesn't exist in the Command Centre or Eurazeo reporting.
3
BU_Initiative__c must be set on every Salesforce opportunity. Without it, the revenue is invisible to the Command Centre. Use: Mining NDT / Pipeline DA / Powerline / Nuclear/OPG / 3X Composite / Base Business.
4
Nuclear has two separate tracks. Vinoth owns OPG MSA/maintenance. Olivier owns Aecon/new build/refurb. Never represent Nucleom in Aecon or SMR conversations without Olivier.
5
AMBER/RED findings = immediate flag. If you or your crew identify an AMBER or RED corrosion finding in the field, flag it to the commercial owner the same day. This is how 3X Composite repair revenue is generated.
