Proposal Health

Open proposals · stalled deals · conversion rates · by initiative
⚡Proposal health figures come from 18 new Salesforce queries Éric adds to the Sunday PA flow (Add-On 1). Three queries run per initiative: one counts all open proposals (Stage = Qualifying, Proposing, Proposal Sent, or Negotiating), one counts stalled proposals with no Salesforce activity in the past 30 days, and one calculates the 90-day conversion rate from Closed Won and Closed Lost deals. Results are written to the seven proposals_* keys in actuals.json every Sunday. Until those queries are added, this section shows dashes.·PENDING — SF queries not yet added to PA flow

Revenue Reconciliation

Salesforce Closed Won vs Business Central invoiced · variance tracking
⚡Reconciliation figures compare two sources side by side: Salesforce Closed Won (from the Sunday PA flow) and Business Central posted invoices (from the BC connector Éric builds as Task P4). A positive variance — where BC is higher than Salesforce — is normal and represents callout and time-and-material work that never enters Salesforce. A negative variance is a data quality flag that Jeremy should investigate. These figures populate the three recon_* keys in actuals.json and will only display once the BC connector is live.·PENDING — live once BC connector (P4) is built

Nuclear Outage Calendar

Jon & Olivier add outage records to actuals.json · Command Centre updates automatically on page load
✏️ Nuclear outage data is maintained manually by Jon Eustace and Olivier Marcotte. When an outage window is confirmed, Jon or Olivier opens actuals.json on SharePoint, finds the nuclear_outages array, copies the blank outage_001 template, and fills in the station, unit, outage type, start and end dates, contract status, estimated value, and notes. Days until the outage and the RAG status (Green ≥90 days, Amber 30–89 days, Red <30 days) are calculated automatically by the Command Centre on every page load — they do not need to be entered manually. There is no automated data feed for this section. Olivier Marcotte is the commercial lead for all Nuclear/OPG activity; Louis-Pier Théberge is the nuclear specialist for legacy Nucleom new builds and refurbishments.
