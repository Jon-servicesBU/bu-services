Live — 4 Active Customers

Nucleom Inc. is the only Canadian company offering ECA through-grease grinding mill gear inspection — already live across 12 active accounts spanning Canadian precious metals, iron ore, oil sands, cement, and international operations. Initiative 3 — Powerline Inspection
LineCore, LineOhm, LineRay — live lines to 735kV — full plan: Powerline Commercial Plan

Powerline Inspection — Summary

Active — RTE France + Hydro-Québec

Nucleom holds three proprietary powerline inspection tools developed through its Hydro-Québec IP partnership — the only complete Canadian live-line transmission inspection suite. RTE (France, Europe's largest HV grid at 100,000+ km) has expressed active interest in powerline inspection services, representing a direct EIG strategic opportunity given EIG's French headquarters and EDF/Eurazeo network.

LineCore — ECA

Zinc layer degradation on ACSR conductors. Live to 735kV. Up to 1.0 m/s. 15–43mm dia. The only live-line ECA conductor tool available in Canada.

LineOhm — Resistance

Micro-ohm splice sleeve resistance. HQ patented. No surface prep. <500g. ~360° articulation. Live to 735kV. Leading indicator of overheating failure.

LineRay — X-Ray

Internal X-ray inspection of splices, connectors, dead ends, dampers. Detects broken strands, voids, contamination. Pairs with LineOhm for full splice assessment.

3X Engineering — The Sustainability BU of Ekoscan Integrity Group

3xeng.com →

3X Engineering is a global leader in composite repair technology for critical industrial assets, founded in 1990 and headquartered in Monaco. 3X Engineering became the Sustainability division of Ekoscan Integrity Group in 2024. 3X Engineering products have been tested with Bureau Veritas, TÜV, ABS, and Lloyd's Register, and are deployed across Oil & Gas, Power, and structural applications in 60+ countries through a network of distribution partners.

BU Services acts as the Canadian delivery partner for 3X Engineering products within EIG — no external distribution agreement is required as both companies are EIG divisions — the only company in Canada combining advanced NDT inspection with the ability to immediately supply and install a certified composite repair solution on defects found during that same inspection. This creates a unique closed-loop offering: inspect, find, fix. No second mobilization. No competing vendor. One proposal, one invoice.

The Strategic Logic Inspect → Find → Fix
Every BU Services inspection that identifies corrosion, cracking, wall thinning, or structural degradation is now a potential 3X composite repair sale. BU Services already has 12 active accounts across mining, oil sands, cement, and international operations — including Barrick Gold, Suncor, Agnico Eagle, ArcelorMittal, Cenovus, and IAMGOLD Suriname — plus active pipeline DA programs at Pembina and Tidewater, powerline clients across Canada, and nuclear/OPG relationships. All of them own assets that can be composite-repaired when defects are found.

The Closed-Loop Inspect-Then-Repair Advantage

Without 3X (Current State)

BU Services finds a defect. Client calls a different repair contractor. Second mobilization. Second invoice. BU Services loses sight of the remediation. No revenue from the repair. Competitor builds a relationship with the client's maintenance team.

With 3X (Initiative 5)

BU Services finds a defect. BU Services immediately quotes a composite repair from the same mobilization. REINFORCEKiT® or TANKiT® installed within days. Single invoice. Deeper client relationship. Higher revenue per visit. No competitor involved.
